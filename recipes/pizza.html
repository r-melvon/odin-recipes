Thin Crust Pizza

Photo by Chad Montano on Unsplash

Description

A thin crust pizza is crisp, light, and perfectly balanced, featuring a delicate, crackly base that lets the toppings shine. bite delivers a satisfying crunch followed by rich sauce, melted cheese, and your favorite toppings without feeling heavy.

  • Crisp, golden-brown crust
  • Light texture with a slight chew
  • Even distribution of toppings in every bite

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • ¼ teaspoon white sugar
  • ¾ cup lukewarm water
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Step 1 Prepare and measure all ingredients.
  2. Step 2 In a small bowl, combine warm water (110°F / 44°C) with yeast and sugar. Let it sit for 5–8 minutes until it becomes foamy.
  3. Step 3 In a large bowl, mix 1 3/4 cups of flour with the salt. Add the yeast mixture and stir until a dough forms.
  4. Step 4 Move the dough onto a floured surface and knead for about 2 minutes until smooth. If the dough feels sticky, gradually add the remaining 1/4 cup of flour.
  5. Step 5 Roll the dough out into a 12-inch circle and place it onto a greased pizza pan.
  6. Step 6 Add your desired toppings, then bake until the crust is golden and cooked through. Serve and enjoy.
